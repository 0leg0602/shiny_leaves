Welcome to SHINY LEAVES!

Thank you for visiting our website, we're excited to share with you our growing collection of fun and engaging games.

  1. How to Play: Explore Games: Start by navigating to the "Games" page. Here, you'll find the Leaf Collector game which challenges your precision and timing skills. Your objective is to collect as many leaves as possible while avoiding their decay (read the game info for more detailed instructions).
  2. What's Next? Once you've collected enough Shiny Leaves, visit the "Shop" to purchase a key. Then, head over to the "Chests" page where you can unlock chests with your key. Inside each chest, you'll find a random gem of varying rarity, shape, and color.
  3. Our Current Status: We're still working on our website to enhance your experience and are excited about the features we have planned. While some parts of our site are still under construction, such as the ability to buy items in the shop, open chests, and interact with gems, you can already start collecting Shiny Leaves, which will unlock further experiences in future updates.
  4. Gem Mastery: The gems you acquire will be displayed on the "Gems" tab. Here, you'll have the opportunity to sell, upgrade, and combine your treasures to create something truly unique!
  5. Stay Tuned! Keep an eye out for upcoming features and enhancements we'll be rolling out soon. We value your feedback and look forward to hearing your thoughts.

Thank you for choosing SHINY LEAVES. Happy collecting!
